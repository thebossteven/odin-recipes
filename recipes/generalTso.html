General Tso's Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 lb chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup corn starch
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • General Tso's Sauce
  1. Cut chicken into 1-inch size cubes.
  2. Toss the chicken with corn starch.
  3. Cook your chicken until all sides are golden brown.
  4. Remove the chicken from the pan.
  5. Saute garlic, ginger and pepper flakes in the pan.
  6. Add the sauce and bring to a simmer then add chicken.
  7. Toss the chicken, making sure to evenly coat each piece. Serve!
